    A high flyer is a student who scores 80 and above in a course taught by a teacher who has 4 star or 5 star rating

    Get the high flyer at a given position

    GET https://highflyers.herokuapp.com/high_flyers/:id

    Rate a teacher as an highflyer

    POST https://highflyers.herokuapp.com/high_flyers/:id/rate/:teacher_id

    Required body payload: rating
